Account and Data Deletion

CarDashCam

DashPilot allows users to delete their account and associated local data directly from the application.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Car Dash Cam app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

When the account is deleted

  • The local user profile is permanently removed.
  • Associated account data stored on the device is deleted.
  • The user is signed out from this device.
